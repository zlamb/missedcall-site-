Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

MissedCall ("we," "our," or "us") is a product of Deal Intel LLC. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our service.

Information We Collect

When you call a business that uses MissedCall and engage in a text conversation, we may collect:

  • Your phone number (provided automatically when you call or text)
  • Your name, address, and service request details (provided by you during the text conversation)
  • Photos or images you send via MMS related to your service request
  • Message content and timestamps

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your missed call with a follow-up text message
  • Qualify your service request and determine urgency
  • Connect you with a technician or service provider
  • Provide the business you called with relevant details to serve you

Information Sharing

We share your information only with the business you originally called so they can fulfill your service request. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to complete the service request and for reasonable business record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

SMS Communication

By calling a business that uses MissedCall, you consent to receiving follow-up text messages related to your service inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your conversation. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted communications and secure data storage.
